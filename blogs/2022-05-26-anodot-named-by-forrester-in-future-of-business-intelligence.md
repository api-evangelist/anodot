<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">It’s hard to believe enterprise BI platforms have been around for three decades.</span> <span style="font-weight: 400;">In that time, they have served the purpose of collecting and analyzing large amounts of data to help businesses make more informed decisions. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">But in today’s data-driven economy, analysts struggle to keep up with the myriad of business intelligence reports from </span><a href="https://www.anodot.com/blog/5-critical-shortcomings-of-traditional-bi-tools/" rel="noopener" target="_blank"><span style="font-weight: 400;">traditional BI tools</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> – which fail to effectively and efficiently analyze and interpret data in real-time. The fact is, traditional BI tools were not designed for the massive volume and speed of data in today’s organizations. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">To address these shortcomings, Forrester recently published </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">The Future of Business Intelligence report. The report gives technology executives recommendations on how to get more value from their BI applications. The recommendations include infusing by platforms with the power of augmented AI and names Anodot as a vendor with these capabilities. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">According to Forrester, many organizations are struggling to get mileage from their current BI applications because they are:</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Not actionable and, therefore, not impactful</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Delivered in silos without context</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Primarily used by data and analytics professionals </span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Forrester’s report focuses on the emerging technologies and techniques that deliver business insights in a more efficient and effective way – beyond </span><a href="https://www.anodot.com/blog/limitations-of-dashboards/" rel="noopener" target="_blank"><span style="font-weight: 400;">data visualizations and dashboards</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">. We’ve captured the top recommendations for technology executives and data leaders.</span></p>
<h3>Close the gap between insights and outcomes with impactful BI</h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Forrester says technology and data leaders must never lose sight of the ultimate BI objective: to effect tangible business outcomes with insight’s driven actions. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">To start closing the gaps between insights and actions, technology and data professionals should: </span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Integrate metadata using advanced tooling</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Leverage digital worker analytics platforms to find links between insights and actions</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Automated operational decisions</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Procure decision intelligence solutions</span></li>
</ul>
<h3><b>Effect tangible business outcomes with actionable BI </b></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">An insight by itself, no matter how informative or revealing, is not actionable. To make BI actionable, Forrester recommends technology and data leaders should: </span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Infuse actions into BI apps</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Embed workflows into analytical applications</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Upgrade business applications with embedded analytics</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Combine analytical and transaction applications via a translytical BI platform</span></li>
</ul>
<h3>Become more effective with augmented capabilities</h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Applying actionable insights is one way to influence business outcomes. To make BI more effective with </span><a href="https://www.anodot.com/blog/what-is-augmented-analytics/" rel="noopener" target="_blank"><span style="font-weight: 400;">augmented capabilities</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">, technology and data leaders should: </span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Adopt an augmented BI platform for AI-infused BI </span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Use the augmented BI platform for anticipatory capabilities</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Forrester recommends </span><b>Anodot’s</b><span style="font-weight: 400;"> AI-driven </span><a href="https://www.anodot.com/learning-center/business-analytics-business-monitoring/" rel="noopener" target="_blank"><span style="font-weight: 400;">business monitoring</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> platform for organizations looking for augmented capabilities. Anodot uses </span><a href="https://www.anodot.com/blog/what-is-ai-ml/" rel="noopener" target="_blank"><span style="font-weight: 400;">AI and machine learning</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> to analyze 100% of business data in real time, autonomously learning the normal behavior of business metrics. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Rather than developing new views, models, and dashboards, teams leveraging</span> <a href="https://www.anodot.com/learning-center/ai-analytics/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">AI analytics</span></a> <span style="font-weight: 400;">gain real-time actionable insights to react to change and predict the future. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Anodot delivers the full context needed for BI teams and business users to make impactful decisions by featuring a robust </span><a href="https://www.anodot.com/learning-center/correlation-analysis/" rel="noopener" target="_blank"><span style="font-weight: 400;">correlation engine</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> that groups anomalies and identifies contributing factors. </span></p>
<h3>BI should be unified and personalized</h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Analytics silos continue to plague companies as they implement different platforms for strategic vs. operational insights, </span><a href="https://www.anodot.com/blog/structured-unstructured-data/" rel="noopener" target="_blank"><span style="font-weight: 400;">structured vs. unstructured</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> data analysis, and basic vs. advanced analytics. Data leaders should invest in options to unify BI as follows: </span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Start by unifying all insights-driven decisions under one umbrella</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Proceed to unify analytics on structured and unstructured data</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Integrate multiple BI platforms via a BI fabric</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Emphasize, prioritize, and invest in BI personalization for different users</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Remove the remaining silos by unifying analytical and operational reporting platforms</span></li>
</ul>
<h3>BI based on adaptive, future fit technology</h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Forrester predicts that firms that prepare for systemic risk events with a future fit technology strategy will outpace competition by growing at 2.7x industry averages. Technology leaders should make future BI adaptive by:</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Architecting to bring BI to data.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Investing in decoupled, headless BI</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Opportunistically deploying full-stack platforms</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;"> Adding investigative intelligence to your future BI tech portfolio mix</span></li>
</ul>
<h3>BI is embedded in all systems of work</h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">According to Forrester, 80% of enterprise decision makers are not using BI applications hands on, rather they are relying on data analytics teams to “bring BI to them”. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">To seamlessly embed relevant insights into all digital workspaces, technology and data leaders must prioritize investments in BI functionality embedded in:</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Business applications</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Enterprise collaboration platforms</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Enterprise productivity platforms</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Browsers </span></li>
</ul>

Unlike dashboards, by using automated machine learning algorithms in an analytics solution like Anodot, you can eliminate business insight latency, and give your business vital information to detect and solve incidents before losing revenue or customers. </span></p>
